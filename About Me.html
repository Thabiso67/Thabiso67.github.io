My Story

I am a disciplined and dependable Computer Science student with a strong interest in cybersecurity and systems technology. I am committed to learning through hands-on practice and consistently work to improve my technical skills by building and documenting practical labs and projects.

I am motivated by continuous learning and enjoy exploring how technology can be used to protect systems and information.
